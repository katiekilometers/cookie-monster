Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024
Effective Date: January 15, 2024

At Chattr, your privacy is important to us. By using our platform, you agree to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this policy. We may update this policy at any time without notice.

  • Personal details (name, email, phone, address, date of birth, photos, contacts, location, device info, browsing history, messages, posts, likes, and more)
  • Social media connections and imported contacts
  • Precise geolocation and device sensor data
  • Payment and financial information
  • Biometric data (where available)
  • Any other data you provide or we collect from third parties

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization of content, ads, and recommendations
  • Analytics, research, and product improvement

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Advertising partners (over 50 companies)

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as we deem necessary, even after you delete your account, to comply with our business needs and legal obligations. Data may be stored indefinitely in backups and archives.

5. Your Choices

  • You may request deletion of your account, but some data may be retained for business or legal reasons.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails by clicking "unsubscribe" (may take up to 90 days to process).
  • Cookie preferences can be managed in your browser, but some cookies are always required.
  • To reject non-essential cookies, go to Settings in the cookie banner (option is not easily accessible).

6. Children's Privacy

Chattr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children, but cannot always verify user age.

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws. By using Chattr, you consent to these transfers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of Chattr means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For questions, email privacy@chattr.com. We may not respond to all requests.
